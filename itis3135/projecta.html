Project A: Charlotte Foods Design Page


Project Overview

  1. My website presents different restaurants in the Charlotte area for client Logan Duvick.
  2. The intended users of the website are Logan Duvick but also for people who wanted to see some restaurants that are more native to the Charlotte area.
  3. The website will provide different restaurants around Charlotte, North Carolina and each webpage will provide a restaurant, some items on their menu, contact information, and a link to the restaurant’s website if the restaurant has a website. The home page will have links to the other webpages and overall introduce the user to the website and restaurants.

Client Information

  1. Logan Duvick
  2. Student of UNCC
  3. lduvick@uncc.edu


Page Design

Home Page

  • The purpose of the page is to introduce the viewer to the website.
  • The audience of the page are people entering the website for the first time and need a starting point.
  • The page will consist of 3 randomly selected restaurant pages spotlighted and easy navigation to each of the pages. There will also be a very short introduction to introduce the website.
  • There is no data entered on this page.
  • No validation is needed.
  • The page will contain links to the other pages as well as contact information at the top.
  • When the user clicks the links to the other pages they will be brought to the other pages. If they click the contact information they will be brought to their email.
  • The spotlighted restaurants will be randomized and require javascript functionality.

The Tipsy Burro Page
